The story

You wait and wait for your piano app to publish new songs. But again, your favorites are still not there!

Now with a single app and a vast available MusicXML files on the net, you wait for no one. MuseTrainer is the only app you need for all the songs out there.

How to play

Download MusicXML files

We recommend musescore.com but you can find it anywhere in the net. We're building a library also.

Plug the MIDI keyboard and play!

For iPhone/iPad and Desktop computers.

Features

  1. Tempo tuning
  2. Measure repeat
  3. Staffs toggle
  4. Practice mode (wait mode)
  5. Listen mode
  6. Virtual keyboard
