The story

You wait and wait for your piano app to publish new songs. But then, your favorites are still not in the latest release.

Now with a single app and a vast available MusicXML files on the net, you wait for no one. MuseTrainer is the only app you need for all the songs out there.

How to play

Download MusicXML files

We recommend musescore.com but you can find it anywhere in the net.

Plug the MIDI keyboard and play!

For iPad and Desktop computers. iPhone is too small, but you can still try.

Features

  1. Tempo tuning
  2. Measure repeat
  3. Staffs toggle
  4. Practice mode (wait mode)
  5. Listen mode
  6. Virtual keyboard
